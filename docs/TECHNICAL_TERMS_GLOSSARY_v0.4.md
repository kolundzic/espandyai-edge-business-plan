# Technical Terms Glossary v0.4

## Edge AI / ivična veštačka inteligencija

AI processing or AI decision support that happens close to the device, sensor, user, or local network instead of only inside a distant cloud system.

Prizemno: pamet se približava uređaju.

---

## ESP32-class device / ESP32-klasa uređaja

A small microcontroller-based device with Wi‑Fi/Bluetooth capability, often used for IoT prototypes and products.

Prizemno: mali čip koji može da se poveže na internet i upravlja senzorima, dugmadima, ekranom, zvučnikom i drugim komponentama.

---

## Firmware / firmver

Software installed directly into the device.

Prizemno: “mali operativni program” koji živi u uređaju.

---

## Device Registry / registar uređaja

A list of known devices, their identity, status, permissions, and owner.

Prizemno: spisak svih naših čvorova i pravila za svaki čvor.

---

## Policy Engine / motor pravila

A system that decides what is allowed, blocked, or requires human approval.

Prizemno: čuvar koji kaže “može”, “ne može”, ili “pitaj čoveka”.

---

## Audit Trail / audit trag

A record of who requested what, when it happened, what the system decided, and what was executed.

Prizemno: dnevnik istine.

---

## OTA Update / bežično ažuriranje

Updating device firmware over the network instead of connecting it manually by cable.

Prizemno: uređaj dobija novu verziju preko interneta.

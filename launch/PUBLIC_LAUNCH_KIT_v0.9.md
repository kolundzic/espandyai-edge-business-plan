## Founder launch message

Today we are introducing the ESPAndyAI EDGE business and production plan.

The idea is simple but powerful:

AI should not remain trapped inside a browser tab.

ESPAndyAI EDGE brings the AndyAI Director system into physical space through small voice and sensor nodes.

A node can listen, speak, log events, connect to Director, and execute approved workflows.

This is not a replacement for human judgment.

It is a new interface for human-governed AI work.

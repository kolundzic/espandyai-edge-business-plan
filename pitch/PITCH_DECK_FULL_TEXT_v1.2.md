## Slide 5 — How It Works

```text
Human speaks
↓
ESPAndyAI EDGE captures event
↓
Director identifies device and context
↓
Policy engine checks permission
↓
Director generates response/action
↓
Node speaks or executes approved action
↓
Event is logged locally and centrally
```

Speaker note:

The device is not pretending to be the entire brain. It is the trusted physical interface. The Director system is the operating intelligence layer.

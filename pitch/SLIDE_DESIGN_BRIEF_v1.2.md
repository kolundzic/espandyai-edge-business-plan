# Slide Design Brief v1.2

## Deck format

Recommended:

```text
10 slides
16:9
clean white background
red/gold accents
large diagrams
minimal text
speaker notes separate
```

---

## Visual rhythm

```text
Slide 1: hero device
Slide 2: problem scene
Slide 3: gap diagram
Slide 4: product system
Slide 5: workflow diagram
Slide 6: use-case grid
Slide 7: business model flywheel
Slide 8: roadmap ladder
Slide 9: moat stack
Slide 10: ask + founder
```

---

## Typography

```text
big headlines
short body text
bold keywords
avoid dense paragraphs
```

---

## Tone

```text
serious
business-ready
technical but understandable
no hype without proof
no fake certainty
```

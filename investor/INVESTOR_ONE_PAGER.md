## Problem

AI is mostly trapped inside screens.  
IoT devices are often simple rule machines.  
Business automation needs physical presence, voice interaction, memory, permissioning, and trust.

## Solution

ESPAndyAI EDGE is a voice-enabled ESP32-class edge node connected to AndyAI Director.

It allows users to speak to a physical device that can:

```text
capture commands
query business knowledge
log events locally
receive Director decisions
speak responses
execute safe local actions
request human approval for risky actions
```

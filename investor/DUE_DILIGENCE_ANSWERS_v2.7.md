# Due Diligence Answers v2.7

## Is this a hardware company or software company?

It is a software-led edge AI platform with hardware entry points.

The hardware creates presence and adoption.

The Director platform creates recurring value.

---

## What is the first product?

The first practical product is the ESPAndyAI EDGE Dev Kit and Business Pilot Pack.

---

## What is the main risk?

The main risks are hardware complexity, unclear first use case, API/voice cost, and support burden.

The mitigation is staged production, narrow pilots, usage caps, and clear workflow scope.

---

## Is the AI running entirely on the ESP32?

No.

The node is a physical interface and safe executor.

Director and model/tool layers provide larger intelligence.

---

## What is the defensibility?

Defensibility comes from the full system:

```text
Director governance
memory/workflow layer
device registry
human approval
audit trail
pilot templates
business deployment process
AndyAI ecosystem
```

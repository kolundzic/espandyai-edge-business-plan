# Investor Narrative v0.8

## Opening

AI has become powerful inside chat windows, but most real-world work still happens outside the browser.

People work in offices, classrooms, workshops, warehouses, labs, hotels, and technical environments.

The next stage of AI adoption is not only better chat.

It is physical presence, trusted action, and workflow integration.

## The thesis

ESPAndyAI EDGE is a physical edge-node product line connected to AndyAI Director.

AndyAI Director is the brain.

ESPAndyAI EDGE Node is the body.

Together, they create a human-governed physical AI interface for real-world workflows.

## Usage Cost Controls

Voice and AI API costs must be controlled.

Recommended rules:

1. Push-to-talk by default.
2. Short spoken answers.
3. Local status beeps where possible.
4. Monthly usage caps.
5. Usage dashboard.
6. Business billing by node or by usage tier.
7. Cache repeated answers.
8. Use cheaper models for simple tasks.
9. Reserve premium models for complex reasoning.
10. Add offline fallback for simple commands.

## Recurring SaaS layer

The device sale validates demand.

The Director subscription creates business value.

Example:

```text
50 customers × $49/month = $2,450 MRR
100 customers × $79/month = $7,900 MRR
300 customers × $99/month = $29,700 MRR
```

MRR means **Monthly Recurring Revenue** — monthly income that repeats if customers stay subscribed.

Prizemno: mesečni prihod koji se vraća svakog meseca.

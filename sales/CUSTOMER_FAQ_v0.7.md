# Customer FAQ v0.7

## Is this just a smart speaker?

No.

A smart speaker is usually a general-purpose consumer assistant.

ESPAndyAI EDGE is designed as a business/workflow node connected to AndyAI Director.

It is focused on:

```text
specific workflows
business documents
device identity
logs
approval gates
custom actions
```

---

## Is the AI inside the small device?

Partly.

The small device handles physical interaction: microphone, speaker, buttons, sensors, local logs, and network connection.

The bigger intelligence lives in AndyAI Director and connected model/tool layers.

---

## Can it work offline?

Some local functions can work offline, such as logging, button events, status display, and predefined actions.

Full AI conversation usually requires connection to Director or an AI service.

---

## Can it access our private documents?

Only if explicitly configured.

A pilot can connect the Director system to selected documents, FAQs, manuals, or approved internal material.

---

## Is it safe?

The system is designed around permission profiles, logs, approval gates, and blocked actions.

Risky actions should require human confirmation.

---

## What is the best first use case?

The best first use case is one narrow workflow where voice interaction saves time:

```text
asking documentation questions
saving notes
recording workshop measurements
sending status events
checking procedure steps
```

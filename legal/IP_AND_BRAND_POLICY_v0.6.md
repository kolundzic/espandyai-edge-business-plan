# IP and Brand Policy v0.6

## Purpose

This project must remain legally clean, original, and clearly separated from external projects.

---

## Core rule

ESPAndyAI EDGE is an independent AndyAI product concept.

It may use ESP32-class hardware and study public open-source ecosystems, but it must not present itself as an official Espressif product or an ESP-Claw derivative.

---

## Naming rule

Do not use external project names as primary product or repository names.

Preferred names:

```text
ESPAndyAI EDGE
AndyAI Director Edge Node
AndyAI Mini Node
AndyAI Edge Device Bridge
```

Avoid:

```text
ESP-Claw AndyAI
OpenClaw ESP device
Espressif AndyAI product
```

---

## Attribution rule

When referencing external open-source projects:

```text
name the source accurately
respect the license
do not imply endorsement
separate inspiration from identity
```

---

## Suggested disclaimer

```text
ESPAndyAI EDGE is an independent AndyAI edge-node concept for ESP32-class devices.
It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Espressif Systems or any external open-source project.
External technologies may be referenced only as ecosystem signals, inspiration, or legally compliant components.
```

---

## Brand identity rule

The AndyAI identity must remain primary:

```text
AndyAI Director
ESPAndyAI EDGE
Japan IT Business
human-governed AI edge platform
```

The project should never look like a copy, clone, or unofficial extension of another brand.

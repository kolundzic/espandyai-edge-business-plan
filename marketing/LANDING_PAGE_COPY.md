## Section — The Problem

AI is powerful, but it usually lives inside a screen.  
IoT devices are useful, but they often lack memory, context, voice, and intelligent workflow control.

Businesses need physical AI access points that can listen, respond, log, and act under human governance.

## Section — The Solution

ESPAndyAI EDGE is a small physical node with microphone, speaker, local logging, Wi-Fi, and Director connectivity.

It can:

```text
capture voice commands
answer from approved knowledge
save notes
send events to Director
trigger safe local actions
request human approval
log activity
```

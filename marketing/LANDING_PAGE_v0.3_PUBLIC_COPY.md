## Short product explanation

ESPAndyAI EDGE is a physical AI node.

It can listen through a microphone, speak through a speaker, store local logs on SD memory, and communicate with the AndyAI Director system.

The device itself is not the whole brain.

The device is the trusted edge node.

AndyAI Director is the bigger operational intelligence layer.

## Problem section

AI is powerful, but most AI still lives inside screens.

IoT devices are everywhere, but most of them are still simple trigger-action machines.

Businesses need something between both worlds:

```text
physical presence
voice interaction
local sensing
safe task execution
business memory
human approval
```

That is the gap ESPAndyAI EDGE is designed to fill.

ESPAndyAI EDGE is designed around human-governed AI.

The system is built to answer these questions:


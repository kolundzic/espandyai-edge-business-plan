## ESPAndyAI EDGE / Director Node

```text

```

ESPAndyAI EDGE gives AndyAI Director a physical voice and presence through small ESP32-class nodes for offices, documentation, education, and workshops.

## Week-by-Week Plan

### Week 1-2 — Signal Lock

- publish/update GitHub repo
- prepare public product definition
- write short LinkedIn announcement
- prepare teaser image
- define landing page sections

### Week 3-4 — Prototype Narrative

- document hardware components
- show first physical assembly
- publish short "building the node" post
- prepare first demo script

### Week 5-6 — First Demo

- record 30-90 second demo
- publish short video
- update landing page
- open waitlist
- start direct outreach to 10-20 contacts

### Week 7-8 — Pilot Offer

- publish pilot offer
- prepare business email template
- contact 20-50 possible pilot partners
- collect objections and questions
- update investor Q&A

### Week 9-10 — Use Case Focus

- choose strongest first vertical
- create one use-case page
- create one case-style fictional demo
- refine pricing

### Week 11-12 — Investor Readiness

- prepare pitch deck
- prepare 90-day progress memo
- summarize traction
- prepare next funding ask
- schedule investor/partner conversations

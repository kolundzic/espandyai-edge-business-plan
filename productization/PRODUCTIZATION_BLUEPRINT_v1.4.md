## Product readiness levels

```text
PRL 1: concept
PRL 2: breadboard demo
PRL 3: enclosed prototype
PRL 4: repeatable prototype
PRL 5: dev kit
PRL 6: pilot batch
PRL 7: commercial-ready
PRL 8: certified/production
PRL 9: scaled product
```

PRL means **Product Readiness Level**.

Prizemno: stepen spremnosti proizvoda za tržište.

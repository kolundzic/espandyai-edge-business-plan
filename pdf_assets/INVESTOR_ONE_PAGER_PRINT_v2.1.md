## The Problem

AI is powerful, but most AI still lives inside screens.

Businesses operate in physical environments: offices, workshops, classrooms, hotels, service counters, labs, and warehouses.

Most IoT devices can sense and switch, but they do not understand workflows, documents, context, approvals, or business memory.

---

## The Solution

ESPAndyAI EDGE connects small physical voice/sensor nodes to AndyAI Director.

```text
ESPAndyAI EDGE Node = microphone, speaker, buttons, SD local log, Wi‑Fi, sensors/actions
AndyAI Director = memory, policy, workflow orchestration, dashboard, audit trail, human approval
```

The device gives AI physical presence.

The Director gives the system operational intelligence.

The human remains the final authority.

# Press and Public Narrative v1.7

## Short public description

ESPAndyAI EDGE is a physical AI edge-node concept that connects small voice/sensor devices to AndyAI Director, allowing people to interact with documents, workflows, and approved automation through a real device.

---

## Medium description

ESPAndyAI EDGE brings AI out of the browser and into physical environments.

A small device can listen, speak, log events, and communicate with AndyAI Director, which provides memory, policy, approval gates, dashboard visibility, and workflow orchestration.

The system is designed for offices, documentation, workshops, education, and custom business deployments.

---

## Long narrative

Most AI systems today live inside screens.

But work does not live only inside screens.

People move through offices, workshops, classrooms, service desks, warehouses, and labs. They ask questions, record notes, check procedures, and need timely answers.

ESPAndyAI EDGE is built around a simple idea:

```text
AI should have a physical interface,
but human authority must remain central.
```

The node is small.

The Director is powerful.

The human remains in command.

# Pilot Pipeline Playbook v2.5

## Pipeline stages

```text
lead
contacted
interested
discovery scheduled
pilot proposal sent
pilot accepted
active pilot
pilot completed
commercial offer
closed won / closed lost
```

---

## Outreach sequence

### Step 1 — Intro

Send short message with one-sentence product definition.

### Step 2 — Use-case question

Ask what documentation, workflow, or physical process is painful.

### Step 3 — Demo concept

Share short demo flow.

### Step 4 — Pilot offer

Send pilot package.

### Step 5 — Discovery call

Select one workflow and define success metric.

---

## Qualification questions

```text
Do they have a physical location?
Do they have repetitive questions/tasks?
Do they have approved documents/procedures?
Can one person own the pilot?
Is there budget for pilot?
Can success be measured in 30–60 days?
```

---

## Pilot rejection reasons

```text
too vague
no budget
no workflow
no decision maker
requires impossible customization
privacy risk too high for first pilot
```

---

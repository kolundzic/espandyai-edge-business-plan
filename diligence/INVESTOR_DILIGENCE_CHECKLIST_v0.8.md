# Investor Diligence Checklist v0.8

## Purpose

This checklist prepares the project for serious investor conversations.

---

## Product evidence

```text
working prototype
demo video
device architecture diagram
Director architecture diagram
pilot use-case definition
technical roadmap
```

---

## Market evidence

```text
edge AI market signal
IoT device growth
voice interface trend
business automation demand
early customer interviews
waitlist or pilot interest
```

---

## Business evidence

```text
pricing model
unit economics
pilot package pricing
SaaS subscription model
hardware margin estimate
sales funnel
```

---

## Technical evidence

```text
firmware plan
device registry plan
security model
Director API plan
logs/audit trail
OTA update roadmap
```

---

## Legal evidence

```text
brand/IP separation
license awareness
disclaimers
privacy assumptions
device safety policy
```

---

## Founder evidence

```text
clear project ownership
Japan IT Business positioning
GitHub repo structure
execution velocity
TAPFORGE version history
```

---

## Investor readiness threshold

The project becomes investor-conversation-ready when at least these are true:

```text
prototype demo exists
landing page exists
investor memo exists
pilot offer exists
financial model exists
clear use cases exist
legal positioning is clean
```

# Partner and Pilot Pack v1.5

## Purpose

This pack is for potential pilot clients, implementation partners, and early business collaborators.

---

## Partner categories

### 1. Pilot Client

A company willing to test ESPAndyAI EDGE in one real workflow.

### 2. Technical Partner

A developer, embedded engineer, or integrator helping build device or Director integrations.

### 3. Channel Partner

A company that can resell or deploy ESPAndyAI EDGE to its customers.

### 4. Strategic Investor

An investor or business partner supporting prototype-to-market development.

---

## Ideal pilot client profile

```text
has repetitive documentation questions
has physical workspace
has small team willing to test
can define one clear workflow
can give feedback within 30–60 days
```

---

## Pilot onboarding steps

```text
1. discovery call
2. workflow selection
3. device placement decision
4. document/FAQ source selection
5. permission rules
6. installation
7. training
8. pilot usage
9. report
10. rollout recommendation
```

---

## Pilot deliverables

```text
configured device(s)
Director dashboard access
workflow map
approved command list
usage report
risk notes
business value summary
next-step recommendation
```

---

## Partner pitch

ESPAndyAI EDGE gives partners a new service category:

```text
physical AI workflow deployment
```

Instead of selling only software, partners can install a visible, useful AI node in the customer’s real environment.

# Founder Demo Script v2.6

## Opening

Thank you for joining.

What I want to show today is not a finished consumer gadget.

It is a practical direction:

```text
AI leaving the browser
and entering real workspaces
through small physical nodes
under human control
```

---

## Product explanation

This small node is the physical interface.

It can listen, speak, log, and send events.

But the bigger intelligence is in AndyAI Director.

Director handles memory, policy, workflow logic, approvals, and audit trails.

---

## Demo sentence

I press the button and say:

```text
AndyAI, save this as a new pilot note for ESPAndyAI EDGE.
```

The node sends the event.

Director processes it.

The node replies.

The dashboard shows the event.

That is the core loop.

---

## Business close

We are not trying to automate everything on day one.

We start with one workflow, one device, one measurable result.

If that works, we expand.

That is the path from prototype to pilot to commercial batch.

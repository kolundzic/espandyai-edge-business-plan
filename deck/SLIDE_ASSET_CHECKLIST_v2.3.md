# Slide Asset Checklist v2.3

## Required assets

```text
hero device render
system architecture diagram
trust workflow diagram
use-case icons
business model flywheel
production ladder chart
founder photo or neutral founder block
Japan IT Business logo or text lockup
GitHub link QR code
contact block
```

---

## Optional assets

```text
prototype photo
desk demo photo
dashboard screenshot
device enclosure sketch
component exploded view
short demo video QR code
```

---

## Slide export targets

```text
PPTX
PDF
image carousel
LinkedIn post sequence
Notion page
investor email attachment
```

---

## Quality rules

```text
one idea per slide
no dense walls of text
diagrams must explain business value
avoid fake claims
avoid overpromising hardware readiness
```
